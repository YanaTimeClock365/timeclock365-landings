Pricing questions

Common questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. All plans start with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. Full access to every feature in your chosen plan during the trial period.

What counts as a "user"?

A user is any employee with an active account in the system — whether they clock in by badge, app, web portal, or any other method. You only pay for active users.

Can I add door access control to an existing plan?

Yes. Door Access Control is available as an add-on at $2.50/user/month (yearly) or $3.00/user/month (monthly). It integrates directly with your existing time tracking data.

Does the hardware cost extra?

Hardware (door readers, biometric terminals) is separate from the software subscription. In many cases you can use existing hardware. We'll confirm compatibility during your demo.

Can I mix plans across locations?

Enterprise plans support custom setups for multi-location deployments. Contact sales to discuss what works for your structure.
