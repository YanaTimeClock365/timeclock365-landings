FAQ

Do we need to replace our existing door hardware?

In most cases, no. TimeClock 365 integrates with the most common access control hardware. We confirm compatibility during your demo before any commitment is made.

How long does setup take?

Most deployments are complete within 1–2 weeks. Cloud-based configuration means no on-site server installation and no extended downtime.

What happens if the internet goes down?

Door readers continue to operate locally during connectivity loss. Records sync automatically when the connection is restored.

Is the system GDPR compliant?

Yes. All door logs and biometric data are handled in accordance with GDPR. We provide data processing agreements and audit-ready documentation.
