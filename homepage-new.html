FAQ

Questions, answered.

Does TimeClock 365 work with our existing access control system?
Yes — we integrate with HID, Suprema, ZKTeco, Paxton, Kisi, Verkada, Gallagher and any controller that emits Wiegand or OSDP. For bespoke setups, our Events API accepts any signed JSON event in under 300ms.
How does biometric clock-in prevent buddy punching?
Our biometric terminals use fingerprint and facial recognition to verify identity at the point of clock-in. Each scan is cryptographically signed and stored in an immutable audit log. With 99% accuracy vs manual methods, proxy check-ins become virtually impossible.
Can employees clock in without internet?
Yes. The mobile app stores clock-in data locally and syncs automatically when the connection is restored. Biometric terminals also buffer events offline. Field crews on remote sites never lose a record.
How does the payroll integration work?
TimeClock 365 connects directly to ADP, Paychex, Gusto, Workday and more. Approved timesheets export in one click, already mapped to your pay codes. No CSV juggling, no manual re-entry.
Is it GDPR and ISO 27001 compliant?
Yes. TimeClock 365 is GDPR aligned with data residency options in the EU and US, and holds ISO 27001 certification. Biometric data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and you can configure retention policies per region.
How long does onboarding take?
Most teams are live in under 48 hours. The web portal and mobile app require zero hardware. Biometric terminals and door readers ship pre-configured — plug in, connect to Wi-Fi, done.
